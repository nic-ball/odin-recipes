Lasagna Bolognese is a classic Italian dish that features layers of pasta, ragu , and bechamel sauce. Here is a detailed recipe for making it at home:

Ingredients

  • Prepare the Ragù.
  • Prepare the Pasta Dough.
  • Prepare the Bechamel Sauce.
  • Parmigiano Reggiano

Instructions

  1. Roll out the pasta dough to a thickness of just over 0.5mm (0.2in) and cut into large rectangles.

  2. Boil each pasta sheet for 30-40 seconds, drain, and place on a tray with a clean dry cloth.

  3. Assemble the lasagna in a greased baking dish by layering béchamel, ragù, and pasta sheets. Add grated Parmesan cheese between layers.

  4. Finish off with a layer of béchamel and a genorous sprinkle of Parmigiano Reggiano.

  5. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.
